# Murkhanchi 100 Lakshane | मूर्खाची १०० लक्षणे
## 100 Signs of a Fool
### From Samarth Ramdas Swami's Dasbodh, Dashak 2, Samas 1

The fool is not one who lacks knowledge, but one who lacks wisdom to apply it. These are the signs to recognize and avoid in ourselves.

1.  **अभ्यासावीण वाद करी** - Argues without study or practice.
2.  **नेणतपणीं सांगे गोष्टि** - Tells stories of things he does not know.
3.  **उपदेशी आपण भ्रष्ट** - Preaches to others while being corrupt himself.
4.  **आपुला अवगुण न पाही** - Does not see his own faults.
5.  **पराव्याचा अवगुण पाही** - Only sees faults in others.
6.  **मागें उणें पुढें भरें** - Speaks ill of others behind their back, praises to their face.
7.  **बोलिला बोल विसरे** - Forgets his own words and promises.
8.  **केले उपकार विसरे** - Forgets favors done for him.
9.  **परावें केले स्मरे** - Remembers only what others did to him.
10. **निंदा आवडे स्तुति नावडे** - Loves to criticize, dislikes praise of others.
11. **आपुली स्तुति आवडे** - Loves his own praise.
12. **क्रोधें न बोले धर्म** - Does not speak of dharma when angry.
13. **व्यसनें जडलीं परम** - Is deeply addicted to vices.
14. **नीतिन्याय सांडी** - Abandons justice and ethics.
15. **अनीतीसी दंडे** - Punishes others without justice.
16. **परपीडा जोडे** - Finds joy in hurting others.
17. **स्वार्थबुद्धी परम** - Is extremely selfish.
18. **परोपकार नेणे** - Does not know how to help others.
19. **आळसें प्रपंच बुडे** - Lets his family life sink due to laziness.
20. **द्रव्यलोभें प्राण सांडे** - Risks his life out of greed for money.
21. **विद्या नाहीं वैराग्य नाहीं** - Has neither knowledge nor detachment.
22. **भक्ती नाहीं ज्ञान नाहीं** - Has neither devotion nor wisdom.
23. **धैर्य नाहीं क्षमा नाहीं** - Has neither courage nor forgiveness.
24. **दया नाहीं शांति नाहीं** - Has neither compassion nor peace.
25. **सत्य नाहीं मृदुता नाहीं** - Has neither truth nor gentleness.
26. **विवेक नाहीं विचार नाहीं** - Has neither discernment nor thought.
27. **संतसंग नावडे** - Dislikes the company of the wise.
28. **दुर्जनसंग आवडे** - Loves the company of the wicked.
29. **काम क्रोध लोभ** - Is filled with lust, anger, and greed.
30. **मद मत्सर दंभ** - Is filled with pride, envy, and hypocrisy.
31. **आपुलें हित नेणे** - Does not know what is good for himself.
32. **परावें हित न साहे** - Cannot tolerate the good of others.
33. **थोरपण मिरवी** - Flaunts false greatness.
34. **लहानपण लपवी** - Hides his own pettiness.
35. **जुनें विसरे नवें धरी** - Forgets the old, clings to the new without reason.
36. **आपण न करी आणिकां करवी** - Doesn't do work himself, makes others do it.
37. **सांगितलें न करी** - Doesn't do what he is told.
38. **न सांगतां आवरी** - Meddles without being asked.
39. **हित सांगे तो वैरी** - Thinks a well-wisher is an enemy.
40. **अहित सांगे तो बरा** - Thinks one who gives bad advice is good.
41. **देवास न मानी** - Does not respect God.
42. **गुरूस न मानी** - Does not respect his Guru.
43. **वडिलांस न मानी** - Does not respect his elders.
44. **ब्राह्मणास न मानी** - Does not respect the learned.
45. **साधूस न मानी** - Does not respect saints.
46. **तीर्थास न मानी** - Does not respect pilgrimage.
47. **व्रतास न मानी** - Does not respect vows.
48. **शास्त्रास न मानी** - Does not respect scriptures.
49. **आपुल्या मतें चाले** - Acts only according to his own whims.
50. **लोकलाज न धरी** - Has no shame before society.
51. **पापास न भिये** - Does not fear sin.
52. **पुण्यास न जाये** - Does not pursue virtue.
53. **नीच संगती धरी** - Keeps low company.
54. **उत्तम संगती सांडि** - Abandons noble company.
55. **कुकर्मीं रत** - Is engrossed in evil deeds.
56. **सत्कर्मीं आळस** - Is lazy in good deeds.
57. **द्रव्यासाठीं लाचार** - Is servile for the sake of money.
58. **मानासाठीं लाचार** - Is servile for the sake of honor.
59. **आपुला दोष लपवी** - Hides his own faults.
60. **पराव्याचा दोष वाढवी** - Exaggerates the faults of others.
61. **न मिळे तरी चोरी** - Steals if he doesn't get what he wants.
62. **मागें पुढें न विचारी** - Does not think of consequences.
63. **आपणास शहाणा मानी** - Considers himself clever.
64. **इतरांस मूर्ख मानी** - Considers others fools.
65. **कळे तरी न करी** - Even if he knows, he doesn't act.
66. **न कळे तरी करी** - Acts even when he doesn't know.
67. **समयीं न बोले** - Does not speak when needed.
68. **असमयीं बडबडे** - Babbles at the wrong time.
69. **प्रश्न न कळे उत्तर देई** - Gives answers without understanding the question.
70. **आपणास सर्व कळे म्हणे** - Claims to know everything.
71. **विद्वानांचा मत्सर करी** - Is jealous of the learned.
72. **अज्ञान्याची संगती धरी** - Keeps company of the ignorant.
73. **थोरांचें उपेक्षा करी** - Disrespects the great.
74. **लहानांस छळी** - Harasses the weak.
75. **बलिष्ठापुढें नमे** - Bows before the powerful.
76. **दुर्बळास दंडे** - Punishes the powerless.
77. **खोटें बोले खरें मानी** - Speaks lies and believes them as truth.
78. **खरें बोले खोटें मानी** - Hears truth and believes it to be a lie.
79. **आपण नासे आणिकांस नासवी** - Ruins himself and ruins others.
80. **देखिलें तें हिरोन नेई** - Steals whatever he sees.
81. **परद्रव्य परस्री अभिलाषी** - Covets others' wealth and wives.
82. **नीतिनें न वागे** - Does not conduct himself ethically.
83. **अनीतिनें संपत्ति जोडे** - Earns wealth through unethical means.
84. **तेणें सुख न माने** - Yet finds no happiness in it.
85. **अखंड चिंता वाही** - Constantly carries worry.
86. **समाधान नाहीं** - Has no contentment.
87. **लोकिकीं नापसंत** - Is disliked in worldly life.
88. **पारमार्थिकीं नापसंत** - Is disliked in spiritual life.
89. **इहलोकीं दुःखी** - Is miserable in this world.
90. **परलोकीं दुःखी** - Is miserable in the next.
91. **जन्म वायां गेला** - Wastes his entire birth.
92. **मेल्यानंतर नरक** - Goes to hell after death.
93. **आपणास धन्य मानी** - Still considers himself blessed.
94. **ऐसा जो मूर्ख** - Such a one is a fool.
95. **त्याचें दर्शन घेऊं नये** - One should not even see him.
96. **त्याची संगती धरूं नये** - One should not keep his company.
97. **त्याचा शब्द ऐकूं नये** - One should not listen to his words.
98. **त्याचा विश्वास धरूं नये** - One should not trust him.
99. **तो मूर्खपणें नासला** - He is ruined by his own foolishness.
100. **आणिकांस नासवील** - And he will ruin others too.

*End of Samas. The wise man examines these signs within himself daily.*
